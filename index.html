HTML+CSS & Marks+Channels

Attributes are the data type used in the visualization above. In regards to the marks utilized, points and areas are used. The points are the locations of each chart on the axes, while the areas are the regions on each of the pie charts. In regards to the channels, this visualization uses position, size and color. The coordinates of the pi charts are both horizontal and vertical position, the size is the change in area of each pie chart, and the color of each region in the pi chart are representative of different variables.
